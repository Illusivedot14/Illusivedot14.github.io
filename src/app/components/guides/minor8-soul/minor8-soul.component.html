How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 90 before approaching this boss as you will get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

This boss will be part of the waves in the Expedition Scenario, alongside Frostspider Queen. To summon this boss, get 6 Blue Stones from either the deposits in front of golem cave (Teleport 4) if you have a chaos pick or above, or the blue stone deposits at Snowy Hills (Teleport 6). Then, talk to the captain of the expedition at Snow Hills (Teleport 6), and bring the stones to the circle surrounded by 3 Prius Magus. You will then be assaulted by waves of Frost Spiders, Frost Skirmishers, and Frostvemon Spiders. Go near the green spiders to trigger their explosion (and run away from them) and use AoE on the other spiders. Near the end of the waves, both Soul of Everfrost and Frostspider Queen will arrive.

Minions

Overall


Depending if he comes first or second will determine your strategy. Don't die or else they'll have a chance to kill the Prius Mages and end the Scenario. Stun whenever he is channeling and use your AOE on his Frozen Souls. If he comes first, focus him down before the Frostspider Queen. If he comes second, focus the Queen.
