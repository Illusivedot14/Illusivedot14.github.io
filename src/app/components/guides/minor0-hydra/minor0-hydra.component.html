How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 50 before approaching this boss as you'll get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

To summon this boss, gather 4 Blue Stones from any Blue Stone Deposit in the Frosty Snowfield (Teleport 6) with any pickaxe or if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above, you can mine directly from the Stone Deposits at the Cave Entrance (Teleport 4). Then bring the stones to the top-right of the Seaside area (Teleport 3), in front of the shipwreck, as shown above.

Overall


This boss is pretty simple as you can just hit and die until the boss is dead. Its spells are pretty easy to avoid due to their long wind-ups. You shouldn't have a problem with this boss.
