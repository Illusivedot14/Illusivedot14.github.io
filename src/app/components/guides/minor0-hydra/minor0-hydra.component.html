How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 50 before approaching this boss as you will get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

To summon this boss, first, gather 4 Blue Stones from any Blue Stone Deposit in the Frosty Snowfield (Teleport 6) or if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above, you can mine directly from the Stone Deposits at the Cave Entrance (Teleport 4). Then, bring them to the top-right of the Seaside (Teleport 3) area, in front of the shipwreck, as shown above.

Overall


This boss is pretty simple as you can just hit and die until the boss is dead. Its spells are pretty easy to avoid due to their long wind-ups. Just be mindful of your surroundings in case mobs come.
