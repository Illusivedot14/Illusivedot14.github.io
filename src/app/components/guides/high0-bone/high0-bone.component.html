How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 100 before approaching this boss as you will get no loot if you're below that threshold. Also if you're not being carried, have at least Mid level gear before attempting to make your life easier.

To find this boss, just go into the Prius Forward Camp (Teleport Q-1) and go north, to the Sealed Land.

Overall


This boss is pretty simple as you can just hit and die until the boss is dead. However, its spells are pretty easy to avoid due to their long wind-ups. Just be mindful of your surroundings in case mobs come.
