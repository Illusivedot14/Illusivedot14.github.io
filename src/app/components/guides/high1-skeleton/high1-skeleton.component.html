Location

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 100 before approaching this boss as you will get no loot if you're below that threshold. Also if you're not being carried, have at least Mid level gear before attempting to make your life easier, from this point on, you need a party.

To find this boss, just go into the Prius Forward Camp (Teleport Q-1) and go north, to the Sealed Land.

Overall


This boss will test your patience if you're melee as you NEED TO STAY AWAY FROM THE BOSS at every 25% threshold. I'd recommend modifying your Health Bar so that you can see every 25%.

Skeleton King starts out with all of his abilities but Absolute Devastation, which he will unlock later. He'll also spawn 2 Skeleton Soliders every 15 seconds, one on each side of the arena. Generally, don't have a lot of units near him as his Drain Soul will give him a ton of healing. He'll use Soul Terror at ranged, clumped up units. There aren't a lot of wind-ups for his abilities, so you have to know the cooldowns to dodge them.

At every 25% HP lost, he'll begin using Ture Power of Despair, which takes 3 seconds to fully power. Once activated, he will instantly kill any player or player-owned unit around him (500 range). So dodge it, or don't if you're that kind of person.

After this point, he'll unlock Absolute Devastation, which he will need to cast at least once during the fight. If he reaches below 0 and he hasn't cast it, he will regain 25% of his health and cast it immediately. Make sure the Skeleton Soliders don't pull you during the ability.

Keep the Solider number down and keep going through the fight and he'll go down.
