How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 100 before approaching this boss as you will get no loot if you're below that threshold. Also if you're not being carried, have at least Demon Lord level gear before attempting to make your life easier.

To summon this boss, first, gather 4 blue stones from any blue stone deposit in the Snowy Hills (Teleport 6) AND 4 green stones from the Fairy Forest (Teleport 9) or if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above, you can mine directly from the stone deposits in front of the golem cave (Teleport 4). Use any pickaxe to gather the stones. Then, go to the Deep Sea and kill Tentacle Lord for the Orb of Deep Sea, which you will need to summon Turtle Lord. Finally, bring all these items to the light east of Tentacle Lord and use the Orb to summon the boss.

Overall


The first thing you should IMMEDIATELY do is lure Turtle Lord to the center of the Deep Sea, above where you killed Tentacle Lord. Since there will be Healing Turtles coming from either side. Use any stuns or interrupts to maintain aggro. Make sure you get someone with a lot of armor to tank Turtle Lord, as his Chomp Armor will make quick work of any glass cannon.

Every forty seconds (or when he reaches full mana), he will use Summon Healing Turtle. Dedicate half your DPS to kill these turtles as soon as possible. When the Healing Turtles reach Turtle Lord, they will heal him for 25% HP.

The only other thing you'll have to worry about in this boss are the Turtles that come whenever Turtle Lord reaches 75%, 50% and 25% HP. Turtle Lord will gain 90% Damage Reduction and will not go below 1 HP until all Turtles are dead. There are eight Turtles that spawn, six with no Damage Reduction, and two with 50% Damage Reduction. A good tip for killing these Turtles quickly is to clump up every 25% HP, as the spawn range of these Turtles are dependent on your positions.


Focus down all the minions as soon as possible and this will be an easy kill.
