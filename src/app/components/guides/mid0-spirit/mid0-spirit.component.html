Location

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 100 before approaching this boss as you will get no loot if you're below that threshold. Also if you're not being carried, have at least Demon Lord level gear before attempting to make your life easier.

To find this boss, just go into the Deep Forest in the Spirit Forest West of Prius (Teleport 9).

Overall


This is your first Mid boss, it is also the easiest Mid boss. One of your main concerns is dodging his Ethereal Strike/Spirit Echo and blocking the Drain Orbs from reaching him, like with Mana Ancient. This boss is straight forward until he reaches 50% HP, where he will use Heaven's Wrath.
