How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 90 before approaching this boss as you will get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

To summon this boss, get 6 red Stones from either the red Stone Deposits at Volcanic Lands (Teleport 8) or in front of the Cave Entrance (Teleport 4) if you have an Abyssal Pickaxeaxe or above. Place these Stones at the skulls East of Wallachia (Teleport 7), below the fort that houses Count and Death Knight, as shown above. A cutscene will play and a portal will appear at East Prius Gate (Teleport 4). MAKE SURE YOU DO NOT KILL THE PORTAL OR YOU WILL UNLEASH A TERRIBLE EVIL. Defend the town for 90 seconds or kill all the demons to summon Lord Beriel.

Overall


Generally, the main strategy is like all the other bosses before, run and die. However, to make the kills faster, make sure to avoid the Meteors that he casts on people. Be sure to keep track of his Destructions, as the third one will stun him for three seconds. Whenever he uses his Catacylsm or Craze, just run away as the footmen will take aggro (both Footmen and Ian are tanky).
