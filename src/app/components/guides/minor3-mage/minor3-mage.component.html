How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 70 before approaching this boss as you'll get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

To summon this boss, gather 4 Red Stones from any Red Stone Deposit in the Volcanic Lands (Teleport 8) with any pickaxe, or, if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above, you can mine directly from the Stone Deposits at the Cave Entrance (Teleport 4). Then, bring the stones to the Blue Obelisk southeast of the expedition in Frosty Snowfield (Teleport 6), as shown above.

Overall


Be mindful of his Blizzard and Dark Star, as those spells will deal a large amount of damage and have a chance to deal even more damage due to Magical Weapon, otherwise, he's pretty simple.
