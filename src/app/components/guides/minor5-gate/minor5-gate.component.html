How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 80 before approaching this boss as you will get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

This boss is the first boss you ecounter in the Avalon Scenario, alongside the three Guardian Angels and Corrupt Angel. To summon these bosses, get 4 White Stones from either the Deposits at the Cave Entrance (Teleport 4) if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above, or the white Stone Deposits at the Duchy of Wallachia (Teleport 7). Then, bring the Stones into the Cave, toward the white Stones at the very end, as shown above. Avalon will appear in front of the Cave Entrance, where you'll have to fight (or run past) Avalon Defenders and Protectors until you reach the Gatekeeper.
