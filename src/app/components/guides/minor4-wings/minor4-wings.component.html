How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 70 before approaching this boss as you'll get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

To summon this boss, gather 4 Red Stones from any Red Stone Deposit in Lava Lands (Teleport 8) with any pickaxe or, if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above, you can mine directly from the Stone Deposits in front of golem cave (Teleport 4). Then, bring the stones above the Ancient Elemental in Lava Land (Teleport 8), as shown above.

Overall


Look carefully at his actions as he does not have any wind-up animation for any of his spells. At 50% HP, just dodge his fireballs as his Dragon's Might only lasts for 5 seconds.
