How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 60 before approaching this boss as you will get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

To summon this boss, first, gather 4 white stones from any white stone deposits in Wallachia (Teleport 7) or, if you have a Chaos Pick or above, you can mine directly from the stones in front of golem cave (Teleport 4). Use any pickaxe to gather the stones. Then, bring them in front of the Church in East Wallachia (Teleport 7), as shown above.

Overall


This boss is also pretty straight-forward. Be careful at 50% HP as he will unleash his True Form for 10 seconds. During this form, he will have increased attack speed and damage. Combined with his Death Bind, he will quickly make short work of you. If you keep your distance during this form until its over, he'll go down like the rest.
