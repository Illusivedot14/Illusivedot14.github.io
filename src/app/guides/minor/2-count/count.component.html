How to Summon

-*

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 60 before approaching this boss as you'll get no experience nor loot if you're below that threshold.

To summon this boss, gather 4 White Stones from any White Stone Deposits at the Duchy of Wallachia (Teleport 7) with any pickaxe or if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above, you can mine directly from the Stone Deposits at the Cave Entrance (Teleport 4). Then, bring the stones in front of the Church in East Wallachia, as shown above.

Overall


This boss is also pretty straight-forward. Be careful when he reaches 50% HP as he will unleash his True Form for 10 seconds. During this form, he will have increased attack speed and damage. He will also unlock Requiem of Blood. If he uses his Death Bind on you during this form, you're pretty much dead. To avoid this, just keep your distance until its over and he'll go down like the rest.
