How to Summon

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 100 AND at least high-level geared before approaching this boss. It's recommended master Valtora first.

To get to this boss, first gather 3 Chaos Magic Stones from killing Chaos Elementals in front of the Elemental Tower (Teleport Q-2) which is also below Golem Cave (Teleport 4), or if you have an Chaos Pickaxe, you can mine directly from the Stone Deposit inside the tower (Teleport Q-2). You'll also need 3 Red Stones from either the Red Stone Deposits at Volcanic Lands (Teleport 8) or in front of the Cave Entrance (Teleport 4) if you have an Abyssal Pickaxe or above. Then bring the stones to the Tinkerer in the center of the tower and craft the Key of Flame, then bring it to the bottom-most teleport to enter Ifrit's Room.

Overall


Ifrit can either be really fast or really slow depending on your team. It's recommended to have cleanses and some damage mitigation spells for this fight.

Ifrit will begin the fight with all of his spells at 90% HP, all of which have easy tells. The first spell he'll cast is Hot Pursuit, which he will cast every 25 seconds. Ifrit will push everyone away and wind up for 1.5 seconds a charge. ALWAYS POSITION YOURSELF SO YOU DON'T LAND IN THE LAVA PITS. These Lava Pits will deal 25% max HP damage per second. He will then pick a random person and charge at them. Please, for the love of god, stand still for at least one second if you're targeted before moving as it will give others the chance to move out of the way. Ifrit will lock on and chase you until he hits the wall. You can dodge the charge by flying, however THE IMPACT KILL INSTA-KILL YOU. Don't stand to the side of him or you'll get Hellfire.

The next spell he'll cast is Flame Meteor, which sends two Meteors to a random location around Ifrit. This spell will more or less always be up throughout the entire fight. Stand diagonal to the meteor to avoid the impact and the waves that will apply Hellfire. These meteors can stack so be careful. The last spell he'll cast is Seed of Flame, this is where you'll need some damage mitigation. Whenever you see a timer on someone, GATHER. You'll need to make sure everyone is in the stack before cleansing. This spell will deal MASSIVE damage to those who are affected by Hellfire.



Alongside Ifrit's spells, you also have to prevent him from attacking. Have one person kite Ifrit while the rest of the party will deal with the mechanics. Ifrit has a chance to dramatically increase his attack speed. Everytime Ifrit attacks he will spawn two Magma which will slowly walk towards Ifrit. On contact they will heal him for 0.5% HP. If there's a lot of them, kill them.


Ifrit's main mechanic is that every 10% HP interval, he will summon two Lava Spirits. These spirits will have a 40 second timer before they will cast Fiery Bullet, which deals small amounts of damage, but they will interrupt their victims for 5 seconds. Ifrit can be trigger on the same 10% HP interval multiple times if he goes beyond 10% above the interval. Kill them ASAP. When they die, they will unleash a wave in a 'X' pattern similar to Flame Meteor.


Lava Spirit Spells


When the boss reaches 75% HP, he will begin his intermission phase, where he will create a giant wheel separated into five sections and two Obsidian Golems. This wheel will last 30 seconds, which is enough for these Golems to get full mana and cast their own Hot Pursuit. DO NOT TOUCH THE WHEEL. If you are not immune to magic this will deal 68139 Magic Damage per second and stunned for 5 seconds. There will always be one Flame Meteor being cast during the wheel as the golems chase you. These golems will start out with 60% to 100% Damage Reduction, depending on the size of the team. This reduction can be broken like a shield. Here are the known amounts for each break per hit:

  • ANY Item's active- 10% per hit
  • Hermit[F] - 10% per hit
  • Knight[W] - 20%
  • Meteor direct impact - 30%
  • Int Dark Knight [RR] - 30% per hit
  • Witch[T-Q] - 30%
  • Lancer[E] - 40%
  • Thunderer[Full Mana R] - 60%
  • LightningMage[F] - 100%

Obsidian Golem Spells


After the wheel, just gather up the Golems and kill them together. That's pretty much how the boss works until it is finished. At 50% max HP, Seed of Flame will now target two people and there will be three Flame Meteor per cast. The next intermission will happen at 25% max HP, where there will also be two Lava Spirits with each Obsidian Golem. You can either kill the Lava Spirits or just ignore them and stun them. Golems will also regenerate mana 50% faster. It is recommended that you just fast burst after the second intermission and then you're done. This boss is a bit of a grind at first as you'll have to kill every Lava Spirit before going into the intermission, but once you've mastered it you'll be able to kill it extremely quickly.
