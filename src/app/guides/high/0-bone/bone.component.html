Location

Make sure that you're AT LEAST Level 100 before approaching this boss as you'll get no loot if you're below that threshold. Also if you're not being carried, have at least Mid Level gear before attempting to make your life easier.

To find this boss, go to the Volcanic Lands (Teleport 8) and go far east, into the Dragon Lair. He'll be in the giant grove in the south.

Overall


As the first High boss, this guy is pretty tough if you're unprepared. You'll require someone who can tank 50% max hp damage every 8 seconds due to his Swipe ability he'll be using all fight. You'll also require good positioning for his Hell Spikes.

Bone Dragon has two Phases: a calm phase, and enraged phase. The duration of his rage phase depends on his mana. So it is important to drain all of his mana during this phase or else he'll go into this mode twice with the remaining mana.

Bone Dragon starts out in his calm phase, where he will have access to the spells: Swipe, Flame Breath, and Petrification. Make sure to have someone to take every Swipe, or else Bone Dragon will wipe the entire team! Be prepared for anything as Bone Dragon can quickly change aggro and turn and swipe. Whenever Bone Dragon says "Freeze!!!", just turn around and you'll be fine, or else you'll be trapped for 10 seconds. If he uses Flame Breath on top of that, you're pretty much screwed as the Flame Spirits will kill you. Flame breath is pretty easy to dodge when unfrozen, just keep moving.

At 50% HP, Bone Dragon will appear in the middle of the arena in his Rage Mode. He'll gain 75% Damage Reduction and gain 2% max HP per second at the cost of 2 mana per second. During this mode, he will be unable to use Petrification. Instead, he'll be able to use Hell Spike the rest of the fight. Make sure your party deals at least 10% max hp to him during this form, so you don't have to deal with this mode twice.


To deal with Hell Spike, place the first two somewhere near the middle of the arena, so there's room to maneuver to hide from the third Hell Spike (see video below). Other than that, once he's off his Rage Mode, he'll gain Petrification again, but keep his Hell Spike. Keep going and he'll be dead in no time.
